More About Noah

After growing up in St. Paul, MN, I earned a dual Bachelor's Degree in Physics and International Studies from Emory University. From there, I worked an economic consulting job in Washington DC where I learned to execute scrupulous data analyses to help big companies' legal efforts. Realizing how effortlessly raw data could be filtered and manipulated to support any story, I lost interest and left the consulting world to embark on a 15 month backpacking adventure across South and South East Asia and East Africa. Upon returning, I completed the Masters of Science in Computational Analysis and Public Policy program from the University of Chicago. With my new skill set, I'm looking to diligently apply data and technology to directly improve lives.
